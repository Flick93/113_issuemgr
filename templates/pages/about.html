About Us

Founded in 2018, Riverstone Analytics transforms complex data into actionable market intelligence for retail businesses. From our headquarters in Minneapolis, our team of 27 data scientists and industry analysts helps mid-sized retailers compete with industry giants by identifying pricing trends, optimizing inventory, and predicting consumer behavior. We're proud that our analytics platform has helped over 300 retailers increase their profit margins by an average of 18%. Our approach combines machine learning with deep retail expertise, developed through our leadership team's 40+ years of combined experience at companies like Target and Best Buy. What sets us apart is our commitment to hands-on support. Every client works directly with a dedicated analyst who understands their unique market challenges. We believe data is only valuable when it drives real business decisions.
